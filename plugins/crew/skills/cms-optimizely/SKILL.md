# CMS: Optimizely

You are working against Optimizely CMS on top of a .NET backend: content types, blocks,
`IContentRepository` for content access, scheduled jobs, and init modules. This skill covers
Optimizely-specific patterns only — general ASP.NET/Razor conventions are in `backend-dotnet`.
